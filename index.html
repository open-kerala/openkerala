💡 What is OpenKerala?

We're not just a community. We're a collective of developers, designers, activists, students, teachers, and everyday citizens coming together to turn local problems into open-source projects that anyone can contribute to, use, or improve.

🚀 Vision

Make Kerala a model for collaborative, open-source-driven problem solving—from the backwaters to the blockchain.

🔓 Open

All code, content, and data are free and accessible to everyone.

🌍 Local First

Every project is designed for Kerala's reality and specific needs.

🧩 How It Works

1

🔍 Identify Real Problems

People submit issues they face—waste, water, roads, education, healthcare, local governance.

2

📋 Curate & Open It Up

We frame issues as open problem statements with local context for collaboration.

3

🛠️ Build Together

Create open-source projects with clear roles for both tech and non-tech contributors.

4

🔄 Share Back

Everything is public. Projects can be reused, improved, or forked by anyone.
